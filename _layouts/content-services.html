What Type of Data Analytics Do You Need?

After years of servicing clients from a variety of business backgrounds, we find that while every client is different, they can generally be categorised into four stages. Knowing which stage you are in your data analytics need can help you focus in developing your capabilities. The Four Stages of Data Analytics Need are STARTUP, LAUNCH, GROWTH, and MATURE.

STARTUP STAGE

oYou ars at this stage if you are still planning your first digital campaign. You are also at this stage if you’ve started digital marketing sometime ago, but have not prioritised tracking and measurement

At the the STARTUP STAGE, you need to prioritise proper implementation of

  • Analytics tracking code
  • Tag management
  • Pixel implementation

You also need to migrate your SEO, SEM and Paid Media cost data to your analytics dashboard

LAUNCH STAGE

Your are at this stage if you have all basic tracking tools implemented. At the LAUNCH STAGE, you have several digital campaigns running, and you’ve started to leverage partners/vendors. At this stage, you need to focus on making sure all the technical setups can withstand actual operational complexities. Ie. Put the system under a stress-test and run QA (Quality Assurance) under various scenarios.

As you get ready for the next stage, also make sure that:

  • External vendors analytics is integrated
  • External vendors analytics is implemented
  • KPIs are aligned with business goals and the overall business objective
  • Goal tracking is set up

GROWTH STAGE

You are at this stage if your goal is -- as the name suggests -- growth. At the GROWTH STAGE, all your analytic tools are already set up and tested under real-life scenarios. This stage about adding muscle to your analytics’ capabilities.

At this stage, you might consider :

  • Shopping cart optimisation
  • Product list management
  • Integration with back-end inventory
  • Integration with front-end CRM databases

MATURE STAGE

You are at this stage if your focus is in getting more granular data from your data analytics. While QA of all measurements continuously run in the background, your analytics need are justifiably more sophisticated. At this stage, your focus should be on solutions such as:

  • Cross-device attribution modelling
  • Cross-media attribution modelling
  • Advanced eCommerce solutions
  • A/B testing
  • Conversion Rate Optimisation

IlmuOne Data has helped clients at all stages of data analytics. If you need help in identifying at which stage you are on the Four Stages of Data Analytics, give us a call. Our team of experts are ready to help.
